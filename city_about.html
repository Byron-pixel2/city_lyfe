About me

The Texas-Born entrepreneur spent 95% of her life in Phoenix, AZ. City always had a knack for writing rhymes ever since she was a little girl but really became seasoned in her craft at the age of 16. She spent years of hard work practicing to ensure that both her lyrics and delivery were unmatchable by being very avid in freestyling, writing, and delivering verse after verse in the private setting of her bedroom.

Where music meets the heartbeat of urban life.

City Lyfe is a rising force in the music scene, blending the essence of modern city living with the timeless power of rhythm and storytelling. Born and raised in the heart of the city, City Lyfe's music reflects the vibrant energy, challenges, and beauty of the streets. With an unmistakable style that draws from hip-hop, R&B, electronic, and indie influences, City Lyfe crafts songs that speak to listeners on a deeply personal level, while keeping the groove undeniable.

As an artist, City Lyfe believes in the power of connection. Whether through introspective lyrics or infectious beats, their music invites you to explore the complex emotions of the modern world—from the struggle for success to moments of celebration, heartache, and self-discovery. Each track is a journey, an invitation to experience the highs and lows of life in the city.

City Lyfe's passion for music started early, with a love for songwriting and experimenting with sounds that blended the old with the new. Over time, this passion transformed into a unique sound that speaks to people from all walks of life. Whether you’re cruising down city streets or chilling at home, City Lyfe’s music creates a soundtrack for every moment.

Join the journey, and let City Lyfe's music be your guide through the city and beyond.
