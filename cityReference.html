City lyfe

connections

City Lyfe has also had the pleasure of working alongside industry signed artist she shared the same stage with. She opened up for Dej Loaf, Lil Durk, SinBad, Dice from LaLas full court life, Lil Mo, Joe Torry, Omar Gooding, hosting parties with appearances by Shay Johnson from Love and Hip Hop ATL, Tee Grizzley, and Rich Homie Quan. She also had the honor of performing for one of the largest Hip Hop venues in Phoenix at the Arizona Hip Hop Festival for hip hop day approved by Mayor Greg Stanton. Radio play and interviews include appearances with 101.1 the Beat.


Insparation

There are several artists that she feels was exemplary in contributing to her sound by exhibiting uniqueness and outstanding leadership. such as: Lauryn Hill, Queen Latifa, MC Lyte, Jay Z, Nas, KRS-One, Run DMC, Funk Master J, Biggie Smalls, and more. The ever going motoivation from her familiy has given City the motivation that its not for the money but for the support that this carrer comes with. Without these talented leaders supporting her music we would have City Lyfe today.

Perfromances

Not only is City Lyfe outstanding when it comes to recording music but is also very entertaining and engaging when it comes to live performances. The registered ASCAP member has performed in many venues in markets such as: San Diego, Los Angeles, New Mexico, Manhattan, Philadelphia, Tucson, Texas and Baltimore. City's music will continue to touch more as she contiues her carrer reaching the hearts of many.
